Payment & Delivery
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and various mobile payment methods.
  • How long does delivery take?
    Deliveries typically take between 2-4 business days. For same-day delivery, please check availability in your area.
  • Is there a delivery fee?
    Yes, a small delivery fee may apply based on your location and order size. This fee is calculated at checkout.
